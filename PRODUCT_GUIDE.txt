                              WHAT IS UNICONSULTING?
================================================================================

UniConsulting is a complete digital platform designed to transform how study 
abroad consulting agencies manage their students. It replaces scattered 
spreadsheets, email chains, and manual document handling with a unified, 
intelligent system that saves time and improves student outcomes.

Think of it as a "mission control center" for your consulting agency - where 
every student's journey from registration to university acceptance is tracked, 
organized, and enhanced with artificial intelligence.


================================================================================
                              WHO IS IT FOR?
================================================================================

PRIMARY USERS:

1. STUDY ABROAD CONSULTING AGENCIES
   - Manage large numbers of students efficiently
   - Track each student's application progress
   - Communicate directly within the platform
   - Use AI to speed up document processing

2. STUDENTS APPLYING TO UNIVERSITIES
   - Complete their profile and documents in one place
   - Get instant AI feedback on their essays
   - Find matching universities based on their scores
   - Stay connected with their assigned consultant

3. TEACHERS/CONSULTANTS
   - View all assigned students in one dashboard
   - Approve new registrations
   - Track application progress with visual boards
   - Communicate via built-in chat
