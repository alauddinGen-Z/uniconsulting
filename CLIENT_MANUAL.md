## ğŸ”§ Troubleshooting

### Chat Shows "Connecting..."

**Cause:** Internet connection issue

**Fix:**
1. Check your WiFi/internet connection
2. Refresh the page (F5)
3. Wait 30 seconds and try again

### Student Can't Log In

**Check:**
1. Is their account **Approved**? (Not pending)
2. Did they verify their email?
3. Try "Forgot Password" on the login page

### AI Features Slow

**Normal:** First AI call may take 10-15 seconds

**If Still Slow:**
1. Check internet connection
2. Try again in a few minutes
3. Contact support if persistent

### Page Shows Error

**Quick Fixes:**
1. Refresh the page (F5)
2. Clear browser cache (Ctrl+Shift+Delete)
3. Try a different browser
4. Contact support with a screenshot
