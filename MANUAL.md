## ğŸ¤– AI-Powered Tools

### Smart Scholarship Matcher

Find scholarships that match your student's profile using our **AI-Powered Search**.

**How to Use:**
1. Go to the student dashboard
2. Click **Scholarships** in the sidebar
3. Type a search like: *"Engineering scholarships in UK for women"*
4. Press **Search**

**Understanding Results:**
- **Match Percentage** - How well the scholarship fits (95% = Excellent match)
- **Amount** - Scholarship value
- **Deadline** - Application deadline
- Click **Apply Now** to visit the scholarship website

### AI Essay Reviewer

Get instant, professional feedback on student essays.

**How to Use:**
1. Open a student's profile
2. Go to the **Essays** tab
3. Click **Get AI Review** on any essay

**Understanding the Feedback:**
| Score | Meaning |
|-------|---------|
| 90-100 | Exceptional - Ready to submit |
| 80-89 | Strong - Minor improvements needed |
| 70-79 | Good - Some revision required |
| 60-69 | Average - Significant work needed |
| Below 60 | Needs major revision |

The AI provides:
- **Critique** - What needs improvement
- **Suggestions** - Specific actions to take

## ğŸ”§ Troubleshooting

### Chat Not Loading?
- **Check your internet connection** - Real-time features require a stable connection
- **Refresh the page** - Press F5 or Ctrl+R
- **Sign out and back in** - This resets your session

### Student Can't Log In?
- Verify their account is **Approved** (not pending)
- Ask them to reset their password via the login page

### AI Features Not Working?
- Check if the essay has enough content (minimum 50 characters)
- Try again in a few moments - the AI may be processing other requests

### Page Shows "Error"?
- Refresh the page
- Clear your browser cache (Ctrl+Shift+Delete)
- Contact support if the problem persists
